Rock Politics is a highly dynamic course, The subscription features a season-based learning approach where the contents change periodically, and the lesson plans are carefully curated to efficiently boost your playing.

You can either Purchase and enroll in a course, Subscribe to RP Dynamic Pro Lessons, or even book a private one-on-one session. Your progress is tracked so you can get realtime feedback. We even offer a free section in the Resources tab that everyone has access to.

RP is an all-in-one platform,a one stop learning hub. There are thousands of guitar courses from hundreds of guitarists online but none have uniformity and universality, they emphasize on what is easier for them to teach, not what lays a solid foundation for your playing. Our plans are affordable and flexible, you can cancel any moment. We make you put in real work, akin to a real course curriculum.
